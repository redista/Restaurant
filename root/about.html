About Us

Welcome to Casa Italiana. Founded in 1987, we are a small restaurant along the Garavogue river. Our top-notch italian cuisine is curated by the skilled chefs here who focus not only on taste and appearance but also experience too! Our fresh fish and shellfish are caught locally and sustainably in waters around Sligo. We hope that you visit us soon.
